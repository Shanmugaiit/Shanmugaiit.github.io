Selected Highlights

Sixteen notable publications from the past four years (2022–2026), spanning photometric stereo, neural rendering, graphics, and generative vision. The complete record follows below.

IEEE/CVF WACV · 2026

Synthesizing Compositional Videos from Text Description

Turns a single sentence into a coherent, multi-object video — composing a scene element by element from plain text.

IEEE/CVF CVPR · 2025

RASP: Revisiting 3D Anamorphic Art for Shadow-Guided Packing of Irregular Objects

Packs irregular 3D objects by the shadows they cast — turning anamorphic illusion into a practical packing algorithm.

Computer Graphics Forum · 2025

TensoIS: A Step Towards Feed-Forward Tensorial Inverse Scattering for Perlin Distributed Heterogeneous Media

Recovers light scattered through cloud-like heterogeneous media in a single feed-forward pass — no slow per-scene optimization.

BMVC · 2025

PanoHair: Detailed Hair Strand Synthesis on Volumetric Heads

Synthesizes strand-level hair on a volumetric 3D head — fine detail where volumetric models usually blur.

BMVC · 2025

Incremental Multi-Scene Modeling via Continual Neural Graphics Primitives

Learns new 3D scenes without forgetting the old — one compact neural model that keeps growing.

IEEE ICASSP · 2025

L3D-Pose: Lifting Pose for 3D Avatars from a Single Camera in the Wild

Lifts 2D poses to 3D and retargets them onto arbitrary avatars in the wild, trained on rigged-avatar synthetic data.

IEEE ICIP · 2025

Deep Unsupervised Despeckling with Unbiased Risk Estimation

Trains SAR despeckling networks with no clean ground truth — an unbiased risk estimator built for multiplicative speckle.

ECCV · 2024

MERLiN: Single-Shot Material Estimation and ReLighting for Photometric Stereo

Estimates material and relights a surface from a single shot — collapsing a multi-image pipeline into one.

SIGGRAPH Asia · 2024

ScribGen: Generating Scribble Art Through Metaheuristics

Reimagines an image as a single expressive scribble — art direction driven by metaheuristic search.

Symposium on Geometry Processing · 2024

Search Me Knot, Render Me Knot: Embedding Search and Differentiable Rendering of Knots in 3D

Searches for and renders true 3D knots by making the entire knot pipeline differentiable.

IEEE/CVF WACV · 2024

GraphFill: Deep Image Inpainting Using Graphs

Fills missing regions by reasoning over graphs, not just pixels — structure-aware image inpainting.

Pacific Graphics · 2023

SS-SfP: Neural Inverse Rendering for Self-Supervised Shape from (Mixed) Polarization

Recovers 3D shape from polarized light with no ground-truth labels — self-supervised shape-from-polarization.

Pacific Graphics · 2023

Hand Shadow Art: A Differentiable Rendering Perspective

Deforms 3D hand models so their cast shadow matches a target image — shadow puppetry via differentiable rendering.

IEEE TPAMI · 2022

Depthwise Spatio-Temporal STFT Convolutional Neural Networks for Human Action Recognition

Replaces heavy 3D convolutions with frequency-domain STFT blocks — lighter networks that still capture motion.

ECCV · 2022

DeepPS2: Revisiting Photometric Stereo with Two Differently Illuminated Images

Brings photometric stereo down to just two differently lit images — far fewer captures than the classic setup.

IEEE/CVF WACV · 2022

Shadow Art Revisited: A Differentiable Rendering Based Approach

Recovers a single 3D sculpture whose shadows match multiple target images from different viewpoints — shadow art via differentiable rendering.

Research Themes Over Time

A bird’s-eye view of the group’s output from 2007 to 2026: each year’s publications are grouped into six broad research themes — tracing the path from early HDR imaging and computational photography, through saliency, symmetry and retargeting, to recent photometric & inverse rendering, differentiable-rendering visual art, neural 3D, and generative vision.
